Dog Breeders

Starting as a new breeder in 2014, by two young passionate dog lovers, Keshuv & Krishna, we eventually made our name in dog breeding. We have been producing quality puppies, and we intend to improve the quality by bringing a new bloodline in the coming years and new dog breeds.
Our aim is to make happy home by providing you with loving companion who is going to fill your home with happiness.
We just launched the name "Kanine Passion" to represent us. The idea behind our kennel's name emanated since we share a long-existing human-canine bond, whereby breeding turned into a passion. Also, simply because of our unconditional love for dogs treated as part of the family.
